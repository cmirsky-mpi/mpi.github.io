  • Dependability

    We put decades of experience to use in order to provide our customers with replacement molded rubber products they can rely on in even the most hostile environments. Our products work effectively in temperature ranges from -50°F to +450°F and in environments from salt water to CO2 and H2S. Our parts outperform the competition even in the most difficult projects.
